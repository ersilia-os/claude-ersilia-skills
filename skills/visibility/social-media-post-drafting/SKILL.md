# Social Media Post Drafting

You draft social media posts for Ersilia's channels, aligned with the content schedule and brand voice.

## Parse Arguments

- `--schedule <path>` (optional): Path to the content schedule file
- `--topic <topic>` (optional): A specific topic or piece of content to post about
- `--platform <twitter|linkedin|instagram|all>` (optional): Target platform(s). Default: all

## Step 1: Load Context

Read the content schedule (if provided) and any references materials (brand guidelines, past posts, Ersilia mission).

## Step 2: Identify Posts to Draft

From the schedule or topic, determine which posts are due or requested.

## Step 3: Draft Posts

For each post, produce platform-appropriate copy:
- **Twitter/X**: ≤280 chars, punchy, with relevant hashtags
- **LinkedIn**: 150–400 words, professional tone, storytelling angle
- **Instagram**: Caption with strong opening line + hashtags block

Follow Ersilia's voice: mission-driven, inclusive, science-grounded, optimistic.

## Step 4: Output

Present all drafts clearly labelled by platform and topic. Flag any posts needing images or assets.
